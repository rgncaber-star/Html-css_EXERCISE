UNFAMILIAR WORDS

Stormin' out
This is a shortened version of "storming" which means to leave a place in an angry or agitated manner.
Damned if i do, damned if i don't
This idiom expresses a situation where any course of action will lead to negative consequences. it conveys a sense of being trapped or facing inevitable criticism.
Skin on Skin
A phrase used to describe close physical contact or intimacy between two people.

MY REFLECTION

In Exercise 4, I learned how to use different HTML elements like lists, images, videos, and audio to create a complete music page. I included the singer’s name, the background of the song, and the lyrics, which helped me practice organizing information using lists. Adding the singer’s picture, the music audio, and a related video clip showed me how multimedia can make a webpage more engaging and enjoyable to view. While making the page, I realized that using these HTML features makes a big difference in how users experience a website because it feels more alive and interactive. Overall, this exercise helped me understand how to combine text and media properly, and it made me more confident in creating webpages that are both informative and fun to explore.
